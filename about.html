Our Story

Dedicated to Infrastructure Excellence

Yantriki Supplier & Consultancy was established with a clear mission — to bridge the gap between quality industrial pipe products and the infrastructure projects that need them most. Headquartered in Bhopal, we serve contractors, government bodies, and engineering firms across India.

The name "Yantriki" reflects our roots in the world of machines and mechanics — a symbol of precision, function, and industrial strength. From water distribution to irrigation systems, our products form the backbone of critical infrastructure that millions of people depend on every day.

We operate with a deep understanding of the technical requirements of modern water infrastructure, and our consultancy arm ensures clients receive not just products, but end-to-end guidance for product selection, specification, and project planning.
